# Personas

## Goal

Select 3-5 personas that fit the actual task instead of using a fixed panel every time.

## Required lenses

Every run should include:

- one strategic or builder lens
- one skeptical or critical lens
- one governance or verifier lens

## Add-on lenses by problem type

### Technical / architecture

Prefer adding:
- architect
- operator
- platform engineer
- implementation lead

### Product / business

Prefer adding:
- product strategist
- commercial lead
- customer advocate
- operator

### Research / discovery

Prefer adding:
- researcher
- skeptic
- synthesizer
- governance reviewer

### Marketing / GTM

Prefer adding:
- marketer
- customer voice
- analyst
- brand/risk reviewer

## Anti-patterns

Avoid:

- dev-only rosters for clearly cross-functional prompts
- generic personas with no stated concern
- fake disagreement where everyone agrees immediately
