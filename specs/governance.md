# Governance

## Purpose

Governance is the final gate before recommendations are presented as the best next move.

## Recommendation labels

- `safe`
- `caution`
- `risky`

## Review dimensions

Check recommendations for:

- legality and compliance
- security and safety impact
- operational realism
- reputational downside
- resource and budget feasibility
- evidence gaps

## Required behavior

If a recommendation is risky:

- do not present it as unquestioned final action
- downgrade it into a safer framing, guarded experiment, or open decision point
- refuse clearly harmful or illegal directions

## Output requirement

Governance must be shown explicitly and separately from final synthesis.
