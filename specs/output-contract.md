# Output Contract

The default output should follow this shape.

## Objective

- inferred task
- assumptions
- requested emphasis

## Selected Personas

A short table with:
- persona
- lens
- primary concern
- why chosen

## Selected Cognitive Modules

A short table with:
- module
- selection method
- why chosen

## Debate

### Round 1
Opening positions.

### Round 2
Challenges, rebuttals, and blind spots.

### Round 3
Convergence only when needed.

## Governance Gate

A table with:
- recommendation
- status
- why
- safer framing / guardrail

## Final Synthesis

- agreements
- disagreements
- recommendation
- open questions
- safe next steps

## Style constraints

- concise but visible debate
- concrete recommendations
- no fake consensus
- no hidden governance
